About IronThread Tech

IronThread Tech was founded to help organizations address the hard middle ground between customer expectations, MSP delivery realities, contract interpretation, and operational execution.

Led by Josh Kendall, Principal Consultant, IronThread brings more than two decades of experience across IT operations, service delivery, deskside support, infrastructure support, field services, and global MSP environments.

The IronThread approach is grounded in a simple belief: strong service delivery requires more than effort. It requires structure, accountability, clear scope, honest communication, and a service model that reflects reality.

The name reflects the mission: connecting people, process, technology, and accountability into a stronger operational fabric.
